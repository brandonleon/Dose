What Dose Does

Everything you need to understand your body

Designed for people managing chronic pain, Dose gives you the data to understand what works, when, and how much.

Quick Session Logging

Tap a method button on the dashboard to log a session in seconds. Supports 8 consumption methods with optional pain tracking, dosage amount, and strain notes.

Pain Journal

Log standalone pain entries at any time, with location, activity context, and a 1–10 scale. Build a picture of pain patterns independent of consumption.

T-Break Tracker

Start a tolerance break with an optional goal. Track your streak, view a 30-day bar chart, get daily motivational notifications, and review past breaks.

Stats & Trends

Visualize consumption trends and pain relief effectiveness by method and strain over any date range — week, month, quarter, year, or all time.

Strain Library

Build a personal library of strains with type, THC/CBD percentages, and notes. Mark favorites for quick access during session logging.

Home Screen Widget

Glance at today's session count and last method from your home screen. Tap to open the quick-log sheet directly — no app launch required.

CSV Export

Export your sessions or pain entries as CSV files to share with a doctor, open in Numbers, or archive locally.

Accent Color Themes

Choose from 13 curated accent colors or pick any custom hex color. Your choice syncs to the widget automatically.

100% Private

All data stays on your device. No accounts, no sync, no cloud. Your health information never leaves your iPhone.

User Guide

Five tabs. One workflow.

Each tab in Dose is purpose-built. Here's what you can do in each one.

Dashboard

Your daily command center

  • At-a-glance summary cards showing today's session count, average pain before, and average pain after
  • Quick-log grid — tap any of 8 method buttons to instantly record a session
  • Detailed log button for adding pain levels, dosage amount, strain, and notes
  • Chronological list of all sessions logged today
  • Tap a session card to view full details or navigate to full history
  • Long-press a session to edit or delete via context menu
  • Pain before → after shown inline on each session card

Pain Journal

Track pain independent of consumption

  • Chronological log of pain entries with color-coded severity indicators
  • Record body location (Lower back, Upper back, Neck, Shoulders, Hips, Legs, Full body, or custom)
  • Tag activity context — Morning, After sitting, After exercise, After work, After sleep, and more
  • Free-text notes for each entry
  • Swipe to delete any entry
Tip: Log pain entries before and during sessions to build the most accurate picture of what's working.

T-Break

Tolerance break tracking and streaks

While on a break

  • Large day-counter showing your current streak
  • Progress bar toward your goal (if a goal was set)
  • Start date and elapsed time displayed
  • Daily motivational notifications at 9 AM for up to 90 days
  • End break button with confirmation dialog

History & insights

  • Last 30 days bar chart showing daily session counts
  • Daily and weekly consumption averages
  • Past T-Break history with duration, start date, and goal
Default goal: 7 days. You can set any number when starting a break.

Stats

Understand trends and what works

  • Date range filter: Week, Month, 3 Months, Year, or All Time
  • Summary: total sessions and pain entries in the selected range
  • Consumption trend line chart with shaded area fill
  • Method effectiveness chart — which method provides the most pain relief, sorted by average reduction
  • Strain effectiveness chart — same breakdown by strain (only shows strains with pain data)
  • Daily summary for the last 14 days showing session count and which methods were used

Home Screen Widget

Always in reach

Add the Dose widget to your home screen for an at-a-glance view of today's sessions — and a one-tap shortcut to log your next one.

  • Small: Today's session count and last consumption method
  • Medium: Session count + last strain + pain delta + Quick Log button
  • Refreshes automatically every 30 minutes
  • Tapping "Quick Log" opens the log sheet without navigating the app
  • Accent color syncs with your in-app theme selection
  • All data read directly from your device — no network required
How to add: Long-press your home screen → tap + → search for Dose → choose Small or Medium.

Privacy

Your data, your device

Dose was built with privacy as a first principle.

No Accounts

No sign-up, no email, no profile. Open the app and start using it.

No Cloud Sync

All data is stored locally with SwiftData. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Works Offline

No internet required. Everything runs entirely on your iPhone.

No Analytics

No crash reporters, usage trackers, or third-party SDKs collect your data.

Export your data anytime. Settings → Export → choose Sessions or Pain Entries → share via any app.
